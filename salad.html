Simple Salad

Having a quick and easy meal for dinner or lunch is a must for busy individuals. I like to have a simple salad with a nice homemade dressing most evenings. Lately, this Vegan Ranch Dressing has been my go-to dressing. Who doesn't love Ranch Dressing afterall?!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Head of Romaine Lettuce (Can substitute any other preferred lettuce if desired)
  • 4-5 Medium Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Medium Red, Orange, or Yellow Bell Pepper (5-6 Sweet Mini Bell Peppers can also be substited here.)

For the Dressing:

  • 2 Sprigs of Fresh Dill
  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 tsp. Pepper
  • 1/8 C. Onion
  • 1/4 C. Fresh or Dried Chives
  • 1/3 C. Veganaise
  1. First, chop up the lettuce, tomatoes, and bell pepper. Combine together in large bowl.
  2. Second, you will want to cut off the lemon peel and then combine all the other ingredients into a blender and blend for about 45 seconds to 1 minutes until creamy.
  3. Drizzle dressing over lettuce, tomato, and bell pepper mixture and voila! Add salt and pepper to taste and enjoy!
