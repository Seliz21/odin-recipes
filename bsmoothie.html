Blackberry Smoothie

Blackberries can support brain health and prevent memory loss, due to antioxidants. They're also high in fiber - one cup containing about 8 grams. This can help promote gut health and manage blood sugar levels.

Ingredients:

  • 2 C. Blackberries (Frozen or Fresh)
  • 1 C. Cherries (Frozen or Fresh)
  • 2 Ripe Bananas
  • 5 Dates
  • 1 Liter Water

Instructions:

  1. Place all the fruit in a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend for about 1 minute on the highest setting. Pour and enjoy!
